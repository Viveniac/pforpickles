Our story

It started with one mango tree.

Every summer, the tree in our backyard gave more raw mangoes than one family could ever eat. So they were washed, cut, salted, spiced and set out in the sun — and the jars travelled to neighbours, cousins, colleagues, anyone lucky enough to ask.

The asking never stopped. P for Pickles is that same kitchen, the same recipes passed down through three generations, now made for everyone — one cloth-tied jar at a time, from our home in Bangalore to yours.

Follow the kitchen on Instagram at @pforpickles_blr.
