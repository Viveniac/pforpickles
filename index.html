Our story

It started with one mango tree.

Every summer the tree gave more raw mangoes than one family could eat. So they were washed, cut, salted, spiced and set out in the sun — and the jars travelled to neighbours, cousins, colleagues, anyone lucky enough to ask.

The asking never stopped. P for Pickles is that same kitchen and the same recipes, now made for everyone by Priya — one jar at a time, from our home in Bangalore to yours.

See the latest batches on Instagram at @pforpickles_blr.
