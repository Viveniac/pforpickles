Our story

It started with one mango tree.

Indian food is incomplete without pickles — their spicy, tangy flavours carry you down the memory lanes our grandmothers built.

Every summer our mango tree gave more raw mangoes than one family could eat, so they were washed, cut, salted, spiced and sun-cured — and the jars travelled to neighbours, cousins, colleagues, anyone lucky enough to ask.

The asking never stopped. P for Pickles is that same kitchen and the same recipes, now made for everyone by Priya — fresh and homemade, from our home in Bengaluru to yours.
